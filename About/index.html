About Our Bot

This bot allows you to see information about Roblox users that generally aren't visible to a typical user. Things like their last online date, previous username count, owned items, verified email status, and so much more.

Creator: Autumn

Not much of a Roblox player but I love interfacing with their API and made some tools I thought players would find helpful. I (somehow) managed to make this entire thing in my freetime while juggling college & work.
